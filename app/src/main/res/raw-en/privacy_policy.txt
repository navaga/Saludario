PRIVACY POLICY

Saludario is a personal support app for medication reminders and health record tracking. It does not provide diagnosis, treatment, or medical advice.

WHAT DATA THE APP USES

The app may process information entered by the user on the device, such as medications, doses, schedules, stock, and health records.

It may also store local app preferences, such as language, onboarding, tutorials, and low stock alert state.

The published version may use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics for basic technical analytics and crash diagnostics.

WHERE DATA IS STORED

Saludario's main functional data is stored locally on the device.

The current configuration excludes the local database and app preferences from Android cloud backup and device-to-device transfer.

WHY DATA IS USED

Data is used to show medication reminders, record doses, manage stock, save health records, keep app preferences, and improve technical stability.

NOTIFICATIONS AND PRIVACY

If the user grants notification permission, the app may show medication reminders and low stock alerts.

On the lock screen, the app tries to show a private and generic notification preview to reduce exposure of sensitive details.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The app may integrate Google Firebase services such as Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics.

These services may process technical device and app information according to their own terms and privacy policies.

YOUR CHOICES

The user can grant or deny notification permission, change language and other preferences inside the app, and delete local data from Android or by uninstalling the app.

IMPORTANT

Saludario is a personal support tool and does not replace professional healthcare.
